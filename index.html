A unique education for your unique child

At EduNest School, we’ve got all the space your child needs to become exactly who they want to be. Located in Albany, Western Australia, GSG welcomes day and boarding students from the Great Southern and beyond. Wide open spaces, Christian values, and evidence-based teaching practice work together to help your child thrive — every day.
