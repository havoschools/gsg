Why work at GSG?

When you choose GSG, you’re choosing a learning community that is focused on helping children grow into kind, thoughtful human beings with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in life.

Teaching and learning is at the heart of everything we do at GSG — it is priority number one for our entire team.

Wondering what that means for you?

  • Culture: Joining a supportive, collaborative team means that your knowledge, experience and contribution are always valued.
  • Learning: In addition to offering engaging learning and professional development opportunities across the year, we encourage you to seek out opportunities that light you up. Where we can, we’ll cover the cost.
  • Benefits: On top of your competitive compensation package, we offer school fee discounts, learning and development opportunities, salary sacrificing options, a staff social club as well as study leave.
  • Wellbeing: Our Employee Assistance programme means you can access discreet psychological support and resources when you need them.
  • Leadership: We know that strong leadership sets the tone for success across the organisation. That’s why we invest in developing dynamic, supportive, human-centred leadership practices that will help you grow.
  • Child protection: GSG is fully committed to the protection of children and we expect all staff and volunteers to share this commitment and adhere to our child protection policies.
